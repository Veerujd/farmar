Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused products in their original packaging. For large equipment, please contact our support team for specific return instructions. Some items may be subject to restocking fees.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services for most of our agricultural equipment. Installation fees vary based on the complexity of the equipment and your location. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and financing options for qualified customers. For large purchases, we also offer payment plans.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within the United States and Canada. For international orders, please contact our customer service team to discuss potential shipping options and associated costs.

About AgriMart

We provide high-quality agricultural equipment to farmers and agricultural businesses worldwide. Our mission is to support sustainable farming practices.
